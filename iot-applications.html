IoT Apps

IoT apps analyse vast volumes of linked sensor data in the cloud using machine learning techniques. IoT applications include a variety of properties, including artificial intelligence, connection, dynamic nature, etc. Smartphone applications, fitness trackers, biometric security systems, and other items are some examples of IoT applications.

Smart Building

A smart building is one that makes use of technology to provide residents with a safe and comfortable environment while enabling efficient and economical resource utilisation. A broad variety of current technology may be used in smart buildings, which are also built in a way that makes it possible to include emerging technologies. Building management systems, artificial intelligence (AI), augmented reality, and Internet of Things (IoT) sensors are a few of the robots and processes that may be utilised in a smart building to regulate and enhance its performance. A smart building could include characteristics like an air quality monitoring system, fire and earthquake detection, structural health detection, intelligent parking, and intelligent security. The new Apple headquarters is a fantastic example of a clever structure.
